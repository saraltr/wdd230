Discover the Coastal Beauty of Southern California

Santa Monica Pier: Situated in Los Angeles, the Santa Monica Pier is a famous landmark that has been showcased in various movies and TV shows. You can enjoy carnival games and rides, explore the aquarium, or take a leisurely stroll along the pier to take in the stunning ocean views. The beach is also a great spot for surfing and volleyball, and we highly recommend stopping by our fruit trucks to refuel with fresh and healthy snacks while enjoying your day in Santa Monica.

Newport Beach: Located in Orange County, Newport Beach is a picturesque coastal town with a wide range of attractions, including beautiful beaches, trendy shops and restaurants, and a bustling marina. It's a great place to surf, paddleboard, or take a sunset cruise.
