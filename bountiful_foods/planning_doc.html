Site Purpose

The purpose of Bountiful Foods website is to provide information about the company's products, services, and mission to potential and existing clients. The website will also highlight the company's commitment to promoting organic and locally grown food, and emphasize the freshness and quality of their products.

Target Audience

The target audience for Bountiful Foods' website includes people who are interested in healthy and organic fruits, particularly those who are located in the coastal areas of California. This audience includes both local residents and tourists who are visiting the area. Local residents who are interested in healthy and organic food options may be attracted to the convenience of Bountiful Foods' storefront in Carlsbad and their food trucks, while tourists may be drawn to the unique and authentic farm-to-table experience that Bountiful Foods offers.

Personas & Scenarios

Personas

Name Age Location
Julie 35 years old Carlsbad

Julie is a 35 year old health-conscious local resident who lives in Carlsbad, California. She leads an active lifestyle and enjoys eating healthy, organic foods and supporting local businesses. She likes to shop at farmers' markets. She is interested in Bountiful Foods because of their commitment to promoting organic and locally grown food, and the convenience of their storefront and food trucks. Julie is likely to visit the Bountiful Foods website to learn more about the company's products, place an order online, and find information about upcoming promotions.

Scenarios

Name Age Location
Lucas 25 years old Idaho

Lucas is a 25 year old traveler who is exploring the coastal regions of California for the first time. He is interested in exploring and trying new experiences. He loves to eat healthy and is excited about trying fresh and healthy food for the summer. He learns about Bountiful Foods from a local resident and is immediately intrigued by their commitment to promoting locally grown and organic food. Lucas is especially excited to visit the company's food trucks and experience their unique offerings firsthand. He visits the Bountiful Foods website to learn more about the company's products and services, and to find information about the locations and hours of operation of their food trucks. He is delighted to discover the "build your own specialty drink" feature on the website, which allows him to create a customized fruit drink to his liking.
