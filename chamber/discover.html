Un village Parisien

A stone's throw from Paris, a few strides from the Bois de Vincennes and its lakes, the city is a haven of peace for walkers. Its cobbled streets with wide sidewalks give pride of place to pedestrians and the tempting storefronts of food shops: pastries, bakeries, caterers, chocolate makers, butchers, fishmongers, markets... Quality products and rare flavors are always there, because the Vincennois are as gourmet as they are gourmet!

Demographics

The vast majority of Vincennes companies are sole proprietorships, since 73% of them have no employees, and 92% are made up of fewer than 10 employees. Out of more than 9,700 companies and among the most represented sectors of activity, there are 14% consulting and auditing companies, 12% building companies (construction, construction, cleaning), 10% businesses (hotels , restaurants, food, clothing, personal services, crafts, etc.), 8% from arts and entertainment, 7% from technological sectors (R&D, printing, graphics, IT, engineering, technical studies, newspaper publishing, software development , telecommunications, etc.), 7% from the health sector and 6% from the financial and real estate sector (insurance, banks, real estate agencies, etc.).
