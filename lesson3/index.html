Site Name

Vincennes Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Vincennes Chamber of Commerce mission is to promote and protect our community economy as a whole by supporting local companies and offering networking opportunities for our members. We provide company training, marketing, development workshops, networking gatherings, and legal counsel. The purpose of our website is to provide information and resources to our members and to everyone whiten our community that want to join our organization.
