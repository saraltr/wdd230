Target Audience

Our target audience consists of business owners and managers, as well as entrepreneurs seeking for assistance with their projects. We also welcome non-members who are interested in learning more about commerce.

Personas


Name Age Job
Emily 28 years old Baker/ Small business owner

Emily is a 32 years old womand who recently started her own business, a bakery specializing in gluent-free pastries. But she lacks the ressources and contacts to make it a success. She has a tight budget and is looking for ways to save money and increase her revenue. She is also looking for ways to connect with suppliers and other local business owners. Emily wants to make sure that her business is operating legally and in compliance with all relevant regulations.


Name Age Job
Louis 45 years old Manager in a retail business

Louis is a 45 years old business owner who runs a retail company that specializes in home furnishings. He has been in the business for 20 years and is constantly seeking for new possibilities for growth. He wants to reach out to new customers, so he's looking for ways to boost his marketing and sales efforts, particularly through digital marketing and e-commerce. He wants to stay up-to-date on the lastets trends and best practices in his industry. He also wants to help his community by advocating for policies that benefit the retail industry. Finally, he wants to improve the way he manages his employees and us looking for ressources on the subject.

Scenarios


  • Emily visits the website and looks through the member directory for suppliers businesses that are looking for new customers. She finds several potention suppliers and contacts them through the website.
  • Louis visits the website and finds information on digital marketing workshops and training opportunities. He is able to signs up for it.
  • Emily has trouble navigating the legal and regulatory landscape of the industry. On the website she is able to find the resources on taxes, permits and other legal requirements. She can also book a virtual or in-person meeting with a member of the legal team for additional questions.
  • Louis researches the chamber's advocacy activities and initiatives on matters that are significant to the business community and joins the online moderator team to contribute.
  • Emily wants to connect with other business owners and entrepreneurs in the area. On the website she finds information on upcoming networking events.
